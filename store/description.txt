See prediction market probabilities for the content you're reading.

ON TWITTER/X
A "Q" button appears on every tweet. Click it to see prediction markets relevant to that tweet.

ON ANY ARTICLE
Click the Quantify icon in your toolbar to find prediction markets relevant to the page you're reading.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Extracts key topics from the tweet or article
2. Searches Manifold, Polymarket, Kalshi, and Metaculus
3. Uses Claude to filter for genuinely relevant markets
4. Shows live probabilities with direct links

BRING YOUR OWN KEY
Quantify uses the Anthropic API for relevance filtering. You provide your own API key (get one at console.anthropic.com). Your key is stored locally and never sent anywhere except the Anthropic API.

PRIVACY
- No data collection, analytics, or tracking
- No accounts or servers
- Your API key stays on your device
- All processing happens in the extension
