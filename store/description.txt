See prediction market probabilities for the content you're reading.

ON TWITTER/X
A "Q" button appears on every tweet. Click it to see prediction markets relevant to that tweet.

ON ANY ARTICLE
Click the Quantify icon in your toolbar to find prediction markets relevant to the page you're reading. Ask follow-up questions in the bar at the bottom.

HOW IT WORKS
An AI agent reads your content, searches Manifold and Polymarket, and iterates until it finds the most relevant markets. Typically 2-3 search rounds.

BRING YOUR OWN KEY
Quantify uses the Anthropic API (BYOK). You provide your own API key (get one at console.anthropic.com). Your key is stored locally and never sent anywhere except the Anthropic API.

PRIVACY
- No data collection, analytics, or tracking
- No accounts or servers
- Your API key stays on your device
- All processing happens in the extension
